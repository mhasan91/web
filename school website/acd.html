Curriculum

Our is designed to prepare students for the AS and A level courses offered by the Cambridge International Examinations (CIE). Students at Hasan's Academy elect to study in one of three core areas:

  • Sciences
  • Social
  • Sciences
  • Business
    • View More
The subjects taught at the A Level are: Accelerated Mathematics, Accounting, Art & Design, Biology, Business Studies, Chemistry, Economics, English Literature, History, Law, Mathematics, Further Mathematics, Physics, Psychology, Sociology, Thinking Skills and English Language. English General is taught at the AS level. All students must take English General or English Language plus any one combination of subject combinations offered in the Science, Social Sciences or Business blocks. Further details of the subject combinations offered for 2013 please refer to the Admissions tab on our website.

Grading System

The grading system at The Hasan's Academy is purposely stringent to encourage the students to strive in their studies and with diligence and academic commitment, reach their full potential. Regular class attendance is required in all courses. In order to remain enrolled in a course, a student must maintain a minimum "D" average. View More

The Hasan's Academy grading system is as follows:

  • A
  • B
  • C
  • D
  • E - course failure; must be successfully repeated to obtain a credit for the course.
Courses must be dropped by by specific dates in order not to be counted for grading
