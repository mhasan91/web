Qurani Duaen

بِسْمِ اللّٰهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ

  1. Waldain K Liye Dua

           

  2. Zalim Se Bachnay K Liye

  5. Buray Khayalaat Say Bachnay K Liye

           

  6. Ilm aur Zehn Ki Taraqqi k Liye

           

  7. Har Qism ki Neymat K Husool K Liye

           

  8. Dunya Aur Akhrat Ki Bhalai K Liye

           

  9. Allah (سُبْحَانَهُ وَتَعَالَىٰ ‎) Ki Khaas Rehmat Hasil Karnay K Liye

           

  10. Maghfirat K Liye

           

  11. Rehem K Liye

           

  12. Sabr K Liye

           

  13. Ek Jamay Dua

           

  14. Allah (سُبْحَانَهُ وَتَعَالَىٰ ‎) Ka Sath Hasil Karnay K Liye

           

  15. Jab Gunah Ka Shoq Peda Ho

           

  16. Jab Maghloob Honay Lago

           

  17. Sualeh Nareena Aulaad K Liye

           

  18. Tamam Fikron Say Azadi K Liye

           

  19. Aham Mushkilaat Ka Hal

           

  20. Ahl o Ayaal K Liye Dua
